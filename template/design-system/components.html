Accessibility baseline

Built into tokens.css, rules in docs/architecture/ui-principles.md: keyboard focus shows a 2px :focus-visible ring (Tab through the controls below to see it); controls keep --size-target-min (24px) hit area; motion collapses under prefers-reduced-motion; palette pairs hold WCAG AA contrast — and color is never the only signal (badges pair color with a text label).
